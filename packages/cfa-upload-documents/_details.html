Proof of Income
Tax documents from 2020 or 2021 (such as Form 1040), or wage documents (such as pay stubs, W-2) for all adults in the household that earn income.

Proof of Residency
Current official document with your name and address such as a valid government ID, housing documents (rent receipts, lease, or mortgage statements), or a recent utility bill.

Proof of Identity
A government-issued photo ID such as a driver's license, identity card, passport or Social Security card.

Types of files
We accept .jpeg, .jpg, .png, .pdf, .bmp, .gif, .doc, .docx, .odt, .ods, or .odp files. Each file is limited to 20MB. The maximum number of files is 20.
